This file contains my recipe for 'Chicken taco rice'

1. 2 chicken breast, cubed (less than 1 inch), cooked to temp in a pan
with vegitable oil.
2. Add chopped green pepper, 1 can of corn and black beans.
3. Stir in 1 packet of taco seasoning and a 1 cup of water.
4. Simmer for 5 minutes on medium heat.
5. Add 1 cup of white instant rice, cover and remove from heat.
6. Stir and serve with chips, shredded cheese of choice and sour cream
(or plain greek yogurt).
